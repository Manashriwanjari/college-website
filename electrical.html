About Department

An Electrical Engineering department focuses on the study, design, and application of electrical systems, equipment, and devices. It encompasses a broad range of areas, from power generation and distribution to telecommunications and control systems. These departments typically offer diplomas in electrical engineering, preparing students for careers in various industries, PSUs and power sector.
